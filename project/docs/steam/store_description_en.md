## Early Access FAQ

### Why Early Access?

SpellLoop has a solid core gameplay loop with 10 mages, 6 biomes, and extensive combat systems. We're entering Early Access to get player feedback on balance, gather wishlist data for new features, and iterate quickly.

### How long will it be in Early Access?

We expect 6-12 months of Early Access, targeting a 1.0 release with expanded content, more languages, and community-driven balance and features.

### What's planned for full release?

- More bosses and enemy types
- Expanded meta-progression (permanent upgrades between runs)
- Daily challenge runs
- Complete localization (FR, DE, ZH, JA, KO)
- More weapon fusions and characters

### Will the price change?

The Early Access price will be lower than the full release price. Early supporters get the best deal.
